## MIGRATION RULES

### Employee Overheads

Employee Overheads is removed.

Legacy references must be deleted or remapped to General Overheads.

### Staff Overheads

Staff Overheads is category-only inside General Overheads.

Do not expose Staff Overheads as a standalone module, output, readiness flag, or Cost Summary input.

### Assets Running Costs

Remove running costs from Assets.

If running_cost_annual still exists during migration:

- mark legacy
- do not include in total_asset_cost_annual
- do not pass to Cost Summary
- migrate to General Overheads where appropriate

### Cost Summary

Cost Summary must consume only:

- total_labour_cost_annual
- total_asset_cost_annual
- total_general_overheads
- total_productive_output
- model readiness outputs

Cost Summary must not consume raw P&L lines.

Cost Summary must not own reconciliation.
