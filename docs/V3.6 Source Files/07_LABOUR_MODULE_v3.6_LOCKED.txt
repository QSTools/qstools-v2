# QS Tools — Labour Module Brief (v3.6 — LOCKED)

STATUS: ACTIVE SOURCE BRIEF
VERSION: v3.6

---

## PURPOSE

Labour is the people cost and productive-hour engine.

It calculates the true annual cost of labour and the productive output available to recover business costs.

---

## SYSTEM POSITION

P&L
↓
General Overheads
↓
Labour
↓
Assets
↓
Module Reconciliation
↓
Model Readiness
↓
Cost Summary

---

## CORE ROLE

Labour owns:

- gross wages
- paid entitlements
- productive hours
- employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- employer ACC levy
- total employer contribution
- total labour cost
- total productive output

---

## INCLUDE

Labour includes:

- hourly wages
- salary wages
- paid leave
- public holidays
- sick leave
- non-productive paid time
- employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- employer ACC levy
- staff productive hours
- labour-only minimum recovery visibility

---

## EXCLUDE

Labour must not include:

- staff overheads
- PPE
- training
- recruitment
- staff welfare
- vehicle running costs
- asset ownership costs
- general overheads
- materials
- subcontractors
- hired equipment
- business pricing strategy

Staff overheads belong in General Overheads as category only.

---

## EMPLOYER CONTRIBUTION FORMULA

total_employer_contribution_annual =
total_employer_kiwisaver_annual
+ total_esct_annual
+ total_acc_levy_annual

---

## TOTAL LABOUR COST FORMULA

total_labour_cost_annual =
total_gross_wages_annual
+ total_entitlements_annual
+ total_employer_contribution_annual

---

## OUTPUT CONTRACT

Labour must output:

- total_gross_wages_annual
- total_entitlements_annual
- total_employer_kiwisaver_annual
- total_esct_annual
- total_acc_levy_annual
- total_employer_contribution_annual
- total_labour_cost_annual
- total_productive_output
- labour_ready

Per-staff outputs may include:

- staff_id
- staff_name
- gross_wages_annual
- entitlements_annual
- employer_kiwisaver_gross
- esct_amount
- acc_cu_code
- acc_rate_percent
- acc_levy_annual
- total_employer_contribution_cost
- productive_hours
- is_active

---

## READINESS RULE

labour_ready = true when:

- at least one active labour profile exists
- total_labour_cost_annual exists
- total_productive_output exists
- total_productive_output > 0
- employer KiwiSaver is included
- ESCT is included
- employer ACC levy is included
- total_employer_contribution_annual exists
- Labour does not expose staff_overheads_total
- Labour does not expose employee_overheads_total

---

## COST SUMMARY HANDOFF

Cost Summary must consume:

- total_labour_cost_annual
- total_productive_output

Cost Summary may display Labour components for transparency.

Cost Summary must not rebuild Labour maths.

Cost Summary must not recalculate ACC.

---

## NOT ALLOWED

Labour must not:

- consume General Overheads totals
- consume Assets totals
- allocate overheads to staff
- perform recovery strategy
- perform pricing
- include staff overheads
- include vehicle running costs
- expose Employee Overheads variables

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Labour v3.6 is the people cost and productive-hour engine. It calculates true Labour cost including wages, entitlements, KiwiSaver, ESCT, and employer ACC levy, then exposes total_labour_cost_annual and total_productive_output to downstream modules.
