# QS Tools — Cost Summary Module Brief (v3.6 — LOCKED)

STATUS: ACTIVE SOURCE BRIEF
VERSION: v3.6

---

## PURPOSE

Cost Summary combines locked upstream cost engines into a single total operating cost burden.

Cost Summary answers:

WHAT does the business cost to run?

It does not answer:

- HOW should the business recover it?
- WHAT revenue did the business generate?
- WHAT direct costs / COGS did the business incur?
- WHAT profit target should be applied?
- WHAT should a quote be priced at?

Those belong to downstream commercial layers.

## CORE ROLE

Cost Summary is a read-only aggregation layer.

It consumes source-of-truth totals from:

- Labour
- Assets
- General Overheads
- Model Readiness

It displays:

- total operating cost burden
- total productive output
- required recovery rate
- model readiness / trust state

Cost Summary is macro-first.

It must show the result clearly before any drilldown detail.

## FORWARD-LOOKING COST RULE

Cost Summary is forward-looking.

It is not a historical reconstruction of the P&L.

Cost Summary uses current / forward-looking upstream module outputs to determine what the business needs to recover going forward.

Past P&L values may inform setup and reconciliation, but they must not override locked module outputs.

---

## ASSET COST RULE

Assets is the source of truth for asset ownership cost.

Cost Summary must consume:

- total_asset_cost_annual

Cost Summary may display:

- total_asset_interest_annual

Cost Summary must not recalculate asset interest.

Cost Summary must not use P&L interest as the source of truth for asset finance.

If the P&L interest category includes asset finance, that P&L value is treated as historical context only.

Asset finance interest must come from the Assets module.

---

## CASH FLOW SEPARATION RULE

Principal repayments are real cash pressure but are not operating cost.

Principal must not feed:

- total_asset_cost_annual
- total_cost_burden
- required_recovery_rate
- Cost Summary calculations

Principal belongs in a future Cash Flow layer.

Cost Summary may not include principal repayments as part of operating cost.

Interest belongs in asset cost.

Principal belongs in Cash Flow.

## FORMULAS

total_people_cost_annual =
total_labour_cost_annual

total_business_overheads =
total_general_overheads

total_business_cost_annual =
total_asset_cost_annual + total_business_overheads

total_cost_burden =
total_people_cost_annual
+ total_asset_cost_annual
+ total_business_overheads

required_revenue =
total_cost_burden

required_recovery_rate =
required_revenue / total_productive_output

If total_productive_output = 0:

required_recovery_rate = 0
warning required.

## PEOPLE COST DISPLAY

Show:

- Gross Wages
- Entitlements
- Employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- Employer ACC Levy
- Employer Contribution Total
- Total People Cost

Source of truth:

total_people_cost_annual = total_labour_cost_annual

Cost Summary may display Labour components for transparency but must not rebuild Labour maths.

Cost Summary must use Labour’s final productive output after entitlement and productivity logic has already been applied.

Cost Summary must not use paid hours, raw working hours, or pre-productivity hours as the recovery denominator.

---

## BUSINESS COST DISPLAY

Show macro lines:

- Asset Cost
- General Overheads
- Total Business Cost

total_business_cost_annual =
total_asset_cost_annual + total_business_overheads

Asset interest may appear inside an Assets drilldown only.

Other / non-asset interest may appear inside a General Overheads drilldown only.

The main Cost Summary view must remain simple and macro-first.

---

## TOTAL COST DISPLAY

Show:

- Total People Cost
- Total Asset Cost
- Total General Overheads
- Total Cost Burden
- Total Productive Output
- Required Revenue
- Required Recovery Rate

Required Recovery Rate is the key Cost Summary output.

It represents:

total operating cost burden ÷ final productive output

---

## COST SUMMARY UI PRINCIPLE

Cost Summary must use:

Results first.
Detail on demand.

Top-level display should prioritise:

- Total Operating Cost
- Total Productive Hours
- Required Recovery Rate

Supporting detail should be available through drilldown / expandable sections.

Cost Summary must not become a detailed P&L review page.

---

## MODEL READINESS DISPLAY

Cost Summary must show Model Readiness state.

If model_ready = false:

- totals may display
- totals must be marked not trusted
- blocking modules must be visible
- warning modules must be visible
- downstream outputs must not be treated as final

If model_ready = true and model_readiness_status = warning:

- totals may be used
- warnings remain visible

If model_ready = true and model_readiness_status = ready:

- totals are trusted

---

## RECONCILIATION RULE

Cost Summary does not own reconciliation.

Cost Summary may display reconciliation status from Module Reconciliation / Model Readiness.

Module Reconciliation owns P&L benchmark comparison.

P&L benchmark totals must not drive Cost Summary calculations.

If mismatch exists:

- show warning / error state from Module Reconciliation
- do not silently ignore
- do not alter Cost Summary totals

---

## REVENUE / COGS BOUNDARY

Cost Summary must not include:

- Revenue
- Direct Costs
- COGS
- Margin Pool
- Gross Margin
- Target Profit
- Quote Margin
- Sale price logic

Revenue / COGS owns:

Revenue - Direct Costs = Margin Pool

Business Summary later compares:

Margin Pool - Total Cost Burden = Net Position

Cost Summary only produces the operating burden side of that comparison.

---

## NOT ALLOWED

Cost Summary must not:

- recalculate Labour
- recalculate ACC
- recalculate Assets
- recalculate asset interest
- recalculate General Overheads
- consume raw P&L lines
- consume P&L benchmark as calculation input
- use P&L interest as the source of truth for asset finance
- consume Employee Overheads
- consume Staff Overheads as a separate total
- consume running_cost_annual
- consume principal repayments
- define recovery strategy
- define pricing
- define capacity
- define cash flow
- consume Scenario outputs
- consume Revenue / COGS outputs
- consume Business Summary outputs

---

## DOWNSTREAM HANDOFF

Cost Summary outputs feed downstream modules.

Required downstream outputs:

- total_cost_burden
- total_productive_output
- required_recovery_rate
- total_people_cost_annual
- total_asset_cost_annual
- total_business_overheads
- total_business_cost_annual
- model_ready
- model_readiness_status

Downstream modules must consume these outputs unchanged.

Cost Summary must not reshape itself for downstream assumptions.

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Cost Summary v3.6 is a read-only, forward-looking aggregation layer that consumes Labour, Assets, General Overheads, and Model Readiness outputs only.

It produces total operating cost burden, total productive output, and required recovery rate without reclassifying, distributing, pricing, modelling, or reconstructing historical P&L values.

Cost Summary defines the cost of operating the business going forward.
