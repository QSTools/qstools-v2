# QS Tools — Cost Summary Module Brief (v3.6 — LOCKED)

STATUS: ACTIVE SOURCE BRIEF
VERSION: v3.6

---

## PURPOSE

Cost Summary combines locked upstream cost engines into a single total cost burden.

Cost Summary answers:

WHAT does the business cost?

It does not answer:

HOW should the business recover it?

That belongs to Recovery Summary.

---

## SYSTEM POSITION

P&L
↓
General Overheads
↓
Labour
↓
Assets
↓
Module Reconciliation
↓
Model Readiness
↓
Cost Summary
↓
Recovery Summary

---

## CORE ROLE

Cost Summary is a read-only aggregation layer.

It consumes source-of-truth totals from:

- Labour
- Assets
- General Overheads
- Model Readiness

It displays the total cost burden and baseline recovery visibility.

---

## REQUIRED INPUTS

### From Labour

- total_labour_cost_annual
- total_gross_wages_annual
- total_entitlements_annual
- total_employer_kiwisaver_annual
- total_esct_annual
- total_acc_levy_annual
- total_employer_contribution_annual
- total_productive_output

### From Assets

- total_asset_cost_annual

### From General Overheads

- total_general_overheads

### From Model Readiness

- model_ready
- blocking_modules
- warning_modules
- blocking_checks
- warning_checks
- model_readiness_status

- employee_overheads_total_annual
- employee_overheads_ready
- staff_overheads_total
- running_cost_annual
- raw P&L lines
- P&L benchmark values as calculation inputs
- Cost Allocation outputs
- Recovery Summary outputs
- Scenario outputs
- active_revenue_model
- active_asset_labour_links

## FORMULAS

total_people_cost_annual =
total_labour_cost_annual

total_business_overheads =
total_general_overheads

total_business_cost_annual =
total_asset_cost_annual + total_business_overheads

total_cost_burden =
total_people_cost_annual
+ total_asset_cost_annual
+ total_business_overheads

required_revenue =
total_cost_burden

required_recovery_rate =
required_revenue / total_productive_output

If total_productive_output = 0:

required_recovery_rate = 0
warning required.

---

## PEOPLE COST DISPLAY

Show:

- Gross Wages
- Entitlements
- Employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- Employer ACC Levy
- Employer Contribution Total
- Total People Cost

Source of truth:

total_people_cost_annual = total_labour_cost_annual

Cost Summary may display Labour components for transparency but must not rebuild Labour maths.

---

## BUSINESS COST DISPLAY

Show:

- Asset Cost
- General Overheads
- Total Business Cost

total_business_cost_annual =
total_asset_cost_annual + total_business_overheads

---

## TOTAL COST DISPLAY

Show:

- Total People Cost
- Total Asset Cost
- Total General Overheads
- Total Cost Burden
- Required Revenue
- Required Recovery Rate

---

## MODEL READINESS DISPLAY

Cost Summary must show Model Readiness state.

If model_ready = false:

- totals may display
- totals must be marked not trusted
- blocking modules must be visible
- warning modules must be visible
- downstream outputs must not be treated as final

If model_ready = true and model_readiness_status = warning:

- totals may be used
- warnings remain visible

If model_ready = true and model_readiness_status = ready:

- totals are trusted

---

## RECONCILIATION RULE

Cost Summary does not own reconciliation.

Cost Summary may display reconciliation status from Module Reconciliation / Model Readiness.

Module Reconciliation owns P&L benchmark comparison.

---

## NOT ALLOWED

Cost Summary must not:

- recalculate Labour
- recalculate ACC
- recalculate Assets
- recalculate General Overheads
- consume raw P&L lines
- consume P&L benchmark as calculation input
- consume Employee Overheads
- consume Staff Overheads as a separate total
- consume running_cost_annual
- define recovery strategy
- define pricing
- define capacity

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Cost Summary v3.6 is a read-only aggregation layer that consumes Labour, Assets, General Overheads, and Model Readiness outputs only. It produces total cost burden and baseline recovery visibility without reclassifying, distributing, or pricing costs.
