PRIMARY RULE:
Business Summary mirrors the current business position. It explains the operating position and bridges P&L evidence to Mirra operating truth. It does not create recovery strategy, rates, allocation groups, readiness trust, stress-test ownership, or final outcome verdicts.

---

# 1. PURPOSE

Business Summary is the current business mirror.

It answers:

What is the business doing now?
What margin does it generate?
What cost burden must that margin carry?
What is left after labour, assets, and overheads?
How does Mirra operating position relate back to the P&L?
Is the current business position positive, negative, or under pressure?

Business Summary explains the current baseline.

It does not decide what to change.

It does not calculate final required rates.

It does not issue the final Business Outcome verdict.

It does not own the diagnostic stress tests.

Revenue Reality owns the margin-after-labour stress test.

Asset Reality owns the productive asset utilisation stress test.

# 4. CORE PRINCIPLE

Business Summary is not the system trust gate.

Business Summary is not the diagnostic stress-test layer.

A negative result is still a valid result.

A deficit does not automatically mean Business Summary is broken.

A deficit means the business position is negative.

Model Readiness decides whether source data can be trusted.

Business Summary explains what the trusted or preview data currently says.

---

# 5. CRITICAL READINESS RULE

Negative business results must not automatically make Business Summary not ready.

Correct:

net_position < 0
→ business_summary_status = negative_position, at_risk, or ready_with_warnings
→ business_summary_ready may remain true if source data is trusted

Incorrect:

net_position < 0
→ business_summary_ready = false
→ downstream pages become preview-only solely because the business is losing money

A bad business result is not the same as bad data.

Model Readiness controls data trust.

Business Outcome later diagnoses consequence.

# 15. FORWARD PROOF

Business Summary must show the forward proof:

Revenue
- COGS
= Material Margin / Margin Pool

Material Margin / Margin Pool
- Labour Cost
= Residual Margin after Labour

Residual Margin after Labour
- Assets
- Overheads
= Net Position

This is the core explanatory chain.

It must be visible in plain language.

This is not the same as the Revenue Reality stress test.

Business Summary shows the full chain.

Revenue Reality isolates the first failure checkpoint.

Business Summary owns:

material_margin

# 19. NEGATIVE RESIDUAL MARGIN AFTER LABOUR RULE

Negative residual_margin_after_labour is also a valid calculated result.

It means:

Margin after COGS is not enough to cover Labour before Assets and Overheads are considered.

It does not mean the calculation is broken.

Correct:

residual_margin_after_labour < 0
→ warning / at-risk explanation
→ source trust remains inherited from Model Readiness

Incorrect:

residual_margin_after_labour < 0
→ data failure
→ Business Summary not ready

Revenue Reality owns the diagnostic explanation of this failure point.

# 20. ACTIVITY DRIVER RULE

Business Summary must be mode-aware.

If business_type = labour_based:

activity_driver_type = hours
activity_driver_label = productive hours
activity_driver_value = total_productive_output
required_recovery_label = Required recovery per productive hour
required_recovery_unit_label = $/hour
current_margin_label = Current margin per productive hour
recovery_gap_label = Recovery gap per productive hour

If business_type = product_based:

activity_driver_type = units
activity_driver_label = annual units
activity_driver_value = total_derived_units_annual or units_sold_annual
required_recovery_label = Required recovery per unit
required_recovery_unit_label = $/unit
current_margin_label = Current margin per unit
recovery_gap_label = Recovery gap per unit

Do not use productive hours as the primary driver in product/unit mode.

Do not use annual units as the primary driver in hours-based mode.

# 21. ACTIVITY DRIVER FORMULAS

Business Summary owns:

required_recovery_per_driver
current_margin_per_driver
recovery_gap_per_driver

Formula:

required_recovery_per_driver =
total_cost_burden / activity_driver_value

current_margin_per_driver =
margin_pool / activity_driver_value

recovery_gap_per_driver =
current_margin_per_driver - required_recovery_per_driver

If activity_driver_value = 0:

required_recovery_per_driver = 0
current_margin_per_driver = 0
recovery_gap_per_driver = 0
business_summary_status = review_required or blocked depending mode and source data

Do not divide by zero.

Do not invent driver values.

# 22. HOURS-BASED INTERPRETATION

For hours-based businesses, Business Summary should explain:

- annual revenue
- COGS / direct costs
- material margin
- total labour cost
- asset cost
- overhead cost
- cost burden per productive hour
- current material margin per productive hour
- gap between margin per hour and required recovery per hour

But Business Summary must not claim that material margin automatically recovers labour or assets.

Revenue Reality tests margin after labour.

Asset Reality tests productive asset recovery pressure.

Recovery Summary later tests separate recovery streams.

---

# 23. PRODUCT / UNIT-BASED INTERPRETATION

For product/unit businesses, Business Summary should explain:

- annual revenue
- COGS / direct costs
- margin pool
- annual units
- margin per unit
- total business cost burden
- required recovery per unit
- recovery gap per unit

Product/unit interpretation:

COGS is consumed by the product.
Margin after COGS must recover labour, assets, overheads, and profit.

Business Summary must not build the full product/unit rate stack.

Rate Builder owns fully burdened unit rates.

- whether the difference is classification, timing, accounting treatment, or operating model treatment

This bridge is essential for trust.

# 36. DIAGNOSTIC HANDOFF RULE

Business Summary should hand off diagnostic responsibility clearly.

If residual_margin_after_labour is negative:

Business Summary may show:

The current model is negative after Labour.

But Revenue Reality owns the diagnostic explanation:

Labour consumes more than the available margin pool.

If total_asset_cost_annual is high or net_position becomes negative after assets:

Business Summary may show:

Assets contribute to the operating burden.

But Asset Reality owns the diagnostic explanation:

Productive assets require a high recovery rate or are underutilised.

If net_position is negative after overheads:

Business Summary may show:

The full operating burden exceeds the current margin pool.

But Cost Allocation / Recovery Summary / Business Outcome own the downstream diagnosis.

# 39. BUSINESS SUMMARY READY CONDITIONS

Business Summary is ready when:

- Business Setup mode exists
- Revenue / COGS totals exist
- Cost Summary totals exist
- activity driver value exists for selected mode
- forward proof can be calculated
- P&L-to-operational bridge can be shown or marked not_available
- Model Readiness is not blocked
- no blocking Business Summary-specific warning exists

Business Summary may be ready_with_warnings when:

- net_position is negative
- recovery gap is negative
- residual margin after labour is negative
- Other Income exists
- non-blocking reconciliation variances exist
- bridge is available but contains review signals
- COG detail is preview-only but high-level P&L totals exist
- source data carries warning state
- Revenue Reality or Asset Reality have pressure signals but trusted source data

Business Summary is blocked when:

- selected mode is missing
- revenue/cogs totals missing
- cost burden missing
- activity driver missing for trusted driver output
- Model Readiness is blocked
- upstream source is invalid
- bridge proof does not reconcile due to calculation error

# 41. WARNING MEANINGS

## business_summary_negative_net_position

Use when net_position < 0.

Blocking:

false if source data is trusted.

Meaning:

The business result is negative, but the calculation may still be valid.

## business_summary_negative_residual_margin_after_labour

Use when residual_margin_after_labour < 0.

Blocking:

false if source data is trusted.

Meaning:

Labour consumes more than the margin pool before assets and overheads are recovered.

Revenue Reality owns the diagnostic interpretation.

## business_summary_zero_activity_driver

Use when selected driver denominator is zero.

Blocking:

true for per-driver calculations.

## business_summary_other_income_present

Use when Other Income affects P&L bridge.

Blocking:

false.

## business_summary_bridge_not_available

Use when P&L-to-operational bridge cannot be shown because required reconciliation data is unavailable.

Blocking:

false if forward proof can still display; true only if bridge is required by the current workflow.

## business_summary_bridge_proof_mismatch

Use when bridge proof rows do not reconcile to Mirra operating position.

Blocking:

true until corrected.

## business_summary_revenue_reality_pressure

Use when Revenue Reality reports labour/margin pressure.

Blocking:

false if Revenue Reality source data is trusted.

## business_summary_asset_reality_pressure

Use when Asset Reality reports productive asset pressure.

Blocking:

false if Asset Reality source data is trusted.

## business_summary_upstream_blocked

Use when Model Readiness blocks trusted output.

Blocking:

true.

The current model shows the business is not covering its full operating cost burden.

The current model shows a positive operating position before further recovery and rate tests.

- a negative result is not automatically bad data

# 67. FINAL PRINCIPLE

Business Summary is the current business mirror.

It shows:

Revenue
minus COGS
minus Labour
minus Assets
minus Overheads

It explains what is left.

It bridges the P&L view to the Mirra operating view.

It does not hide bad results.

It does not turn a bad result into a data failure.

It does not decide what to change.

It makes the difference between accounting profit and operating reality clear.

It hands off diagnostics to Revenue Reality and Asset Reality.

No assumptions.
No hidden bridge.
No source mutation.
No false readiness failure from negative results.
No recovery strategy.
No rates.
No verdict.
No duplicate stress-test ownership.
No leaks.
