BUILD STEP 3 — READINESS LOGIC

Each group should expose:

- is_rate_ready
- has_labour_rate
- has_asset_rate
- minimum_recoverable_rate_per_hour

Readiness rules:

Ready:
Has at least one labour rate or asset rate and total rate > 0.

Incomplete:
No labour and no asset rate.

Labour missing:
Asset exists but selected staff/labour rate missing.

Asset missing:
Labour exists but no asset selected.


BUILD STEP 4 — PRODUCTIVE LABOUR TYPE SELECTION REFINEMENT

Current Operational Groups still selects individual staff.

This works, but the better model is to select:

productive labour type

rather than an individual person.

Reason:

The operational group needs a weighted labour type rate, not a specific person rate.

Future target UI:

Required labour type:
Productive — $63.42/hr

Required assets:
PC55 — $6.74/hr

This avoids confusion when multiple operators exist at different rates.
