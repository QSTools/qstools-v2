## MIGRATION RULES

### Employee Overheads

Employee Overheads is removed.

Legacy references must be deleted or remapped to General Overheads.

---

### Staff Overheads

Staff Overheads is category-only inside General Overheads.

Do not expose Staff Overheads as a standalone module, output, readiness flag, Cost Summary input, Quote Engine input, or Feedback Loop input.

---

### Assets Running Costs

Remove running costs from Assets.

If running_cost_annual still exists during migration:

* mark legacy
* do not include in total_asset_cost_annual
* do not pass to Cost Summary
* migrate to General Overheads where appropriate

---

### Cost Summary

Cost Summary must consume only:

* total_labour_cost_annual
* total_asset_cost_annual
* total_general_overheads
* total_productive_output
* model readiness outputs

Cost Summary must not consume raw P&L lines.

Cost Summary must not own reconciliation.

Cost Summary must not consume quote, accepted snapshot, or monthly actuals data.

---

### Revenue / COGS

Revenue / COGS owns:

Revenue - Direct Costs = Margin Pool

Revenue / COGS must not:

* calculate Cost Summary
* calculate Net Position
* calculate quote gaps
* calculate recovery requirements
* treat gross margin as final profit
* consume Labour, Assets, or General Overheads internals

---

### Business Summary

Business Summary owns:

Margin Pool - Operating Cost Burden = Net Position

Business Summary must not:

* test individual quotes
* create repair levers
* store accepted quote snapshots
* mutate Revenue / COGS
* mutate Cost Summary
* become Business Modelling

---

### Business Modelling

Business Modelling owns scenario copies.

Business Modelling must not:

* mutate Business Summary
* mutate Cost Summary
* mutate Labour
* mutate Assets
* mutate General Overheads
* mutate Revenue / COGS
* price quotes
* store accepted quote snapshots
* perform feedback-loop comparison

---

### Quote Engine

Quote Engine owns quote-level leak detection.

Quote Engine must not:

* rebuild Cost Summary
* rebuild Labour
* rebuild Revenue / COGS
* rebuild Business Summary
* mutate Business Modelling scenarios
* treat gross margin as proof of profit
* overwrite accepted quote snapshots
* consume monthly actuals

---

### Accepted Quote Snapshots

Accepted quote snapshots must be immutable.

Draft and revised quote versions may change.

Accepted versions must freeze.

Only accepted quote snapshots enter the future Feedback Loop.

---

### Feedback Loop

Feedback Loop owns prediction-vs-reality comparison later.

Feedback Loop must not:

* overwrite accepted quote snapshots
* overwrite monthly actuals
* mutate source modules automatically
* perform job-level costing in V1
* act like machine learning
* invent rules

## NOT ALLOWED

The codebase must not:

* leave active employee_overheads contracts
* expose Staff Overheads as standalone downstream output
* let running_cost_annual feed Assets total or Cost Summary
* let Cost Summary own reconciliation
* let Revenue / COGS calculate Net Position

* let Business Modelling mutate source modules
* let Quote Engine rebuild upstream maths

* hide model readiness trust state

## FINAL DEFINITION

The v3.7 migration removes legacy overhead structures, separates reconciliation from Cost Summary, preserves the trust gate, and ensures downstream modules only consume clean, approved source-of-truth variables.

It additionally protects the new commercial chain:

Revenue / COGS → Business Summary → Business Modelling → Quote Engine → Accepted Quote Snapshot → Feedback Loop later

so prediction, baseline, scenario, and monthly reality remain cleanly separated.
