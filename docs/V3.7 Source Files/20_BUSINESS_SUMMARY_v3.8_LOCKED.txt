# 1. PURPOSE

Business Summary is the current business mirror.

It combines the trading result from Revenue / COGS with the operating cost burden from Cost Summary.

Business Summary answers:

```text
Is the current business generating enough margin pool to carry its operating cost burden?
```

It answers that question through the selected business activity driver:

```text
productive hours for labour-based businesses
units sold for product-based businesses
```

Business Summary is the locked current-reality baseline for the Recovery Chain.

It does not answer:

```text
how should recovery be split?
what should the user change?
how should the user price?
what scenario should the business run?
what quote should be accepted?
what cash-flow pressure exists?
did a specific job perform correctly?
```

Those belong to later layers.

---

# 2. PRODUCT ROLE

Business Summary is not a quote tool.

Business Summary is not a modelling tool.

Business Summary is not a recommendation engine.

Business Summary is not Recovery Summary.

Business Summary is not Cost Allocation.

Business Summary is not Business Outcome.

Business Summary is the macro current-state mirror.

It tells the user:

```text
This is what the business currently looks like when trading margin is compared against operating cost burden.
```

For labour-based mode it shows this through hours.

For product-based mode it shows this through units.

# 4. RECOVERY CHAIN PRINCIPLE

The recovery chain must follow this separation:

```text
Business Summary = current reality
Recovery Summary = chosen strategy + visible consequence
Cost Allocation = delivery structure + dependency
Business Outcome = verdict
```

Core product rule:

```text
You can choose anything, but you cannot hide from what it causes.
```

Business Summary provides the current-reality side of that rule.

Recovery Summary provides the choice and consequence side.

# 5. CORE ROLE

Business Summary is a factual mirror.

It shows:

```text
Revenue - Direct Costs = Margin Pool
```

Then:

```text
Margin Pool - Operating Cost Burden = Net Position
```

Then it shows the activity-driver reality of the model:

```text
Required Recovery Per Driver
vs
Current Margin Per Driver
=
Recovery Gap Per Driver
```

The driver is:

```text
productive hours in labour_based mode
units sold in product_based mode
```

This page shows the business as it currently stands.

It does not tell the user what to change.

# 6. UNIVERSAL BUSINESS SUMMARY PRINCIPLE

The shared recovery equation is:

```text
Total Business Cost ÷ Activity Driver = Required Recovery Per Output
```

Where:

```js
activity_driver_value = productive hours OR units sold
```

Business Summary must use this shared activity-driver concept rather than duplicating separate page logic.

# 11. CORE FORMULAS

## Net Position

```js
net_position = margin_pool - total_cost_burden
```

Where:

```text
margin_pool comes from Revenue / COGS
total_cost_burden comes from Cost Summary
```

## Activity Driver Value

Labour-based:

```js
activity_driver_value = total_productive_output
```

Product-based:

```js
activity_driver_value = units_sold_annual
```

## Required Recovery Per Driver

```js
required_recovery_per_driver = activity_driver_value === 0
  ? 0
  : total_cost_burden / activity_driver_value
```

If `activity_driver_value = 0`, warning required.

## Current Margin Per Driver

```js
current_margin_per_driver = activity_driver_value === 0
  ? 0
  : margin_pool / activity_driver_value
```

If `activity_driver_value = 0`, warning required.

## Recovery Gap Per Driver

```js
recovery_gap_per_driver = current_margin_per_driver - required_recovery_per_driver
```

If `activity_driver_value = 0`, warning required.

## Legacy Current Margin Per Productive Hour

```js
current_margin_per_productive_hour = total_productive_output === 0
  ? 0
  : margin_pool / total_productive_output
```

Warning required when in `labour_based` mode and `total_productive_output = 0`.

## Legacy Recovery Gap Per Hour

```js
recovery_gap_per_hour = current_margin_per_productive_hour - required_recovery_rate
```

Warning required when in `labour_based` mode and `total_productive_output = 0`.

# 15. REALITY BASELINE RULE

Business Summary is the baseline reality layer.

The baseline is not a historical P&L ratio.

The baseline is:

```text
required recovery per driver
current margin per driver
recovery gap per driver
margin pool
total cost burden
net position
```

P&L history is processed upstream into Revenue / COGS and Cost Summary.

Business Summary receives those processed outputs and converts them into current commercial reality.

Recovery Summary must not go back to raw history.

# 47. FINAL DEFINITION

Business Summary is the factual current-business mirror and locked current-reality baseline for the Recovery Chain.

It combines Revenue / COGS Margin Pool with Cost Summary Operating Cost Burden to show Net Position and recovery pressure through the selected activity driver.

For labour-based businesses, the driver is productive hours.

For product-based businesses, the driver is units sold.

It answers:

```text
Is the current business generating enough margin pool to carry its operating cost burden?
```

It does not advise, model, price, repair, allocate, decide, or optimise.

It hands a clean current-reality contract to Recovery Summary so the next page can show:

```text
Reality → Strategy → Consequence
```

without rebuilding upstream maths.
