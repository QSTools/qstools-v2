# QS Tools — QuickStart Flow Brief (v1.1 — LOCKED)

## PURPOSE

The QuickStart flow is designed to:

* guide users into the system without friction
* meet users where they currently think (job, m², or hourly)
* lead them to a single realisation point
* trigger the question:

> “What does this actually mean for my business?”

---

## POSITIONING (CRITICAL)

The QuickStart is:

* a guided awareness journey
* the front door to QS Tools

The QuickStart is NOT:

* a full system
* a final answer
* a pricing solution

---

## CORE CONCEPT (LOCKED)

> Start where the user is → show what the job produces → reveal what it actually means

---

## FLOW STRUCTURE (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### Entry Points (3)

* Square Metre Rate Calculator
* Materials + Labour Calculator
* Labour Reality Calculator

---

### Primary Guided Flow

* Square Metre → Labour Reality
* Materials + Labour → Labour Reality

---

### Core Rule

> All entry paths must direct the user to Labour Reality

---

## CALCULATOR ROLES

---

### 1. Square Metre Calculator (ENTRY)

**User mindset**
“I price based on output”

**Purpose**

* show what the job produces per hour

**Output**

* gross / implied hourly rate

**Messaging**

“This is what your job setup is producing.”

**CTA**

“Check what this actually means → Labour Reality”

---

### 2. Materials + Labour Calculator (ENTRY)

**User mindset**
“I price jobs based on materials + labour”

**Purpose**

* show job-based outcome converted to hourly

**Output**

* gross / implied hourly rate

**Messaging**

“This is your gross working outcome from this job.”

**CTA**

“Check what this really means → Labour Reality”

---

### 3. Labour Reality Calculator (CORE — TRUTH ENGINE)

**User mindset**
“I want to understand what this means in reality”

**Purpose**

* convert perceived rate into actual working rate

**Includes**

* non-productive time
* entitlements
* downtime

**Output**

* adjusted working rate

---

## CRITICAL MESSAGING (MUST INCLUDE)

Labour Reality must clearly state:

* This rate includes non-productive paid time
* This is NOT take-home pay
* Tax, ACC, and employer obligations are NOT included

---

## KEY USER JOURNEY (LOCKED)

Step 1
User enters numbers in entry calculator

Step 2
User sees strong gross hourly rate

Step 3
User is prompted to check Labour Reality

Step 4
User sees reduced working rate

Step 5
User recognises the gap

---

## DESIRED USER REALISATION

> “I thought I was earning $X… this is what it actually means in reality.”

---

## END STATE

User asks:

> “What does this mean for how my business should be set up?”

---

## ROLE OF EACH LAYER

### Entry Calculators

* Hook
* Familiar starting point
* Show “what the job produces”

---

### Labour Reality

* Truth engine
* Creates awareness
* Reveals gap

---

### Full System (QS Tools Core)

* Solution layer
* Models the business
* Defines required recovery
* allows validation of real jobs later

---

## UX RULES

* Keep flow simple and linear
* Always show next step clearly
* Do not overwhelm with inputs
* Do not calculate everything
* Focus on clarity over completeness

---

## NOT ALLOWED

* No tax calculations
* No ACC calculations
* No full business modelling
* No duplication of labour logic
* No “final answer” outputs

---

## DEFINITION OF DONE

QuickStart is complete when:

* all three calculators exist
* entry calculators link to Labour Reality
* Labour Reality clearly shows reduced rate
* messaging is consistent across pages
* user flow is obvious and simple
* user reaches realisation moment

---

## DEFINITION

The QuickStart flow is a multi-entry guided experience that starts from how users currently think about pricing their work and leads them to a Labour Reality view that exposes the gap between perceived and actual outcomes, creating the need for the full QS Tools system.

---

## FINAL LINE

> QuickStart shows what your work produces. QS Tools shows what your business actually requires.
