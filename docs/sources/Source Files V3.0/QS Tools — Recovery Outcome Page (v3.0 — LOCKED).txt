# QS Tools — Recovery Outcome Page (v3.0 — LOCKED)

STATUS: FINAL SYSTEM SUMMARY (BUSINESS REALITY LAYER)

---

## PURPOSE

The Recovery Outcome page brings together all system outputs into a single, clear view of how the business is actually performing.

It exists to:

* mirror the business back to the user
* combine financial, pricing, and operational realities
* show whether the business is working, tight, or not working
* provide clarity without prescribing decisions

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

> We do not tell the user what to do. We show them what is happening.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Recovery Outcome is the **final summary layer before real-world validation**.

Position in system:

Cost Summary
↓

↓
Recovery Summary
↓
Rate Models
↓
Production Capacity
↓
**Recovery Outcome**
↓
Quote Benchmark

### 2. Pricing Position

### 3. Production Capacity

* Production Capacity

* time required
* time available
* utilisation
* intensity

---

## OUTPUT STRUCTURE (LOCKED)

The page must present a **clear, structured summary** of:

---

### 1. Financial Reality

* required revenue vs achieved structure
* overall recovery position

Purpose:

> Does the business generate enough to support itself?

---

### 2. Pricing Reality

* required rates vs model assumptions

Purpose:

> Is the business charging correctly?

---

### 3. Production Reality

* time required vs available
* utilisation level
* intensity / pressure

Purpose:

> Can the business realistically deliver the required output?

---

### 4. Overall Business Status

Single clear status:

* ✔ Working
* ⚠ Tight
* ❌ Not Working

---

## STATUS LOGIC (LOCKED)

The overall status must reflect:

* financial viability
* pricing alignment
* production capacity

---

### ✔ Working

* revenue achievable
* rates aligned
* capacity available or comfortable

---

### ⚠ Tight

* revenue achievable but fragile
* rates or capacity under pressure
* limited margin for error

---

### ❌ Not Working

* revenue not realistically achievable
* underpricing or insufficient recovery
* capacity constraint or unsustainable intensity

---

## INSIGHT LAYER (IMPORTANT)

The page should include:

* observations
* implications
* pressure points

Examples:

* “This model relies on very high utilisation”
* “Your rates are aligned, but delivery capacity is tight”
* “Revenue target exceeds realistic production capacity”

---

## CRITICAL RULES

### 1. No Prescriptive Advice

The system must NOT:

* tell the user what to do
* recommend specific actions
* assume business decisions

---

### 2. Reflect, Do Not Direct

The system must:

* present the situation clearly
* allow the user to interpret and decide

---

### 3. No New Calculations

This page must:

* use outputs from existing modules
* not introduce independent logic

---

## USER EXPERIENCE

The user should feel:

* clarity
* recognition
* understanding

Not:

* confusion
* judgement
* instruction

---

## WHAT THIS PAGE DOES NOT DO

* does not generate quotes
* does not adjust inputs
* does not simulate scenarios
* does not replace earlier modules

---

## DEFINITION

The Recovery Outcome page is the final system layer that reflects the complete financial, pricing, and operational reality of the business, allowing the user to clearly understand whether their model works before applying it to real-world quotes.
