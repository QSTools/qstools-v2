# QS Tools — Recovery Summary Page (v3.1 — LOCKED)

STATUS: STRATEGY / REVENUE DESIGN LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

The Recovery Summary Page defines:

> how the business will recover its required revenue

It exists to:

* convert required revenue into recovery streams
* allow the user to design their business model
* show the implications of that design clearly

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

> The user chooses how the business earns — the system shows what that means

---

## SYSTEM ROLE (CRITICAL)

Recovery Summary is:

strategy layer

Position:

Cost Summary
↓
Business Model
↓
Recovery Summary
↓
Rate Models

---

## INPUTS

From Cost Summary:

* required_revenue

---

## USER INPUTS

### Recovery Allocation (%)

User must define:

* labour_recovery_percentage
* materials_recovery_percentage

Optional:

* other_recovery_percentage

---

## RULE

* total must equal 100%
* system must enforce completion
* no automatic distribution

---

## CALCULATIONS

### Recovery Required Per Stream

labour_recovery_required =
required_revenue × labour_recovery_percentage

---

materials_recovery_required =
required_revenue × materials_recovery_percentage

---

other_recovery_required =
required_revenue × other_recovery_percentage

---

## OUTPUTS

* labour_recovery_required
* materials_recovery_required
* total_recovery_required

---

## STRATEGY VISIBILITY (NEW — CRITICAL)

The page must clearly show:

### 1. Revenue Composition

* how much revenue must come from each stream

---

### 2. Relative Dependency

* % reliance on labour
* % reliance on materials

---

### 3. Balance Indicator

The system must classify:

* Labour-heavy
* Balanced
* Materials-heavy

---

## INSIGHT PANEL (GUIDED, NOT PRESCRIPTIVE)

The page must include structured guidance:

---

### Labour-heavy Model

* Higher reliance on labour margin
* More sensitive to time overruns
* Requires strong productivity control

---

### Materials-heavy Model

* Higher reliance on material margin
* Sensitive to pricing pressure
* Dependent on supply chain and markups

---

### Balanced Model

* Risk distributed across streams
* More stable recovery structure
* Less sensitive to single failure point

---

## NO-AUTOMATION RULE

This page must NOT:

* optimise percentages
* suggest exact values
* override user input

---

## COMPLETION RULE

Page is complete only when:

* allocation = 100%

---

## CORE RULES

* no cost logic
* no rate logic
* no benchmarking
* no feedback into Cost Summary

---

## DEFINITION

The Recovery Summary Page v3.1 is the strategy layer of QS Tools. It converts required revenue into user-defined recovery streams and makes the structure and implications of that decision visible without optimising or altering it.
