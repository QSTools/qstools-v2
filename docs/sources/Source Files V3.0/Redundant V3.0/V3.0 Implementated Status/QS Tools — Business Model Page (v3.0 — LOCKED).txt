# QS Tools — Business Model Page (v3.0 — LOCKED)

STATUS: REALITY MIRROR + INSIGHT ENGINE

---

## PURPOSE

The Business Model Page compares:

* actual business performance
  vs
* required business performance

It exists to:

* mirror the business back to the user
* reveal structural issues
* highlight strengths and weaknesses
* create awareness before modelling begins

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

> This page explains what is happening — it does not tell the user what to do

---

## SYSTEM ROLE (CRITICAL)

The Business Model Page is:

diagnostic + insight layer

Position:

P&L
↓
Cost Summary
↓
Business Model
↓
Recovery Summary

---

## INPUTS

### From P&L

* total_revenue
* materials_cost
* subcontract_cost
* total_cogs

---

### From Cost Summary

* required_revenue
* total_cost_burden

---

## CALCULATIONS

### Gross Profit

gross_profit =
total_revenue - total_cogs

---

### Recovery Gap

recovery_gap =
total_revenue - required_revenue

---

### Recovery Ratio

recovery_ratio =
total_revenue ÷ required_revenue

---

## OUTPUTS

* gross_profit
* recovery_gap
* recovery_ratio

---

## INSIGHT ENGINE (CRITICAL)

The page must generate structured insights based on system state.

---

### MODE 1 — UNDER RECOVERY

Trigger:
recovery_ratio < 1

Insights:

* Business is not recovering full cost base
* Revenue is insufficient relative to structure
* Ongoing operation will reduce profitability
* Risk of hidden loss despite activity

---

### MODE 2 — CLOSE / TIGHT

Trigger:
recovery_ratio ≈ 1

Insights:

* Business is operating near break-even
* Low margin for error
* Small changes in cost or output may impact performance
* Structure is fragile

---

### MODE 3 — STRONG RECOVERY

Trigger:
recovery_ratio > 1

Insights:

* Business is covering its cost base
* Surplus is being generated
* Structure appears sustainable
* Opportunity to reinvest or stabilise

---

## STRUCTURAL INSIGHTS (IMPORTANT)

The system must also highlight:

### 1. Revenue vs Cost Balance

* proportion of revenue consumed by business costs

---

### 2. Dependency Signals

Examples:

* heavy reliance on labour
* low gross margin
* high overhead burden

---

### 3. Pressure Indicators

Examples:

* reduced material margin
* increased reliance on labour contribution
* tight recovery buffer

---
