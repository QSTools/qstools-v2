# QS Tools — Recovery Summary Page (v3.1 — LOCKED)

STATUS: CORE SYSTEM MODULE (BUSINESS STRUCTURE LAYER)

---

## PURPOSE

The Recovery Summary page defines how the business is structured to recover its costs and generate revenue.

It exists to:

* show how different parts of the business contribute to revenue
* define how costs are covered through work performed
* clarify the relationship between cost structure and earning structure
* establish the logic that feeds into Rate Models

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

> This page defines how the business is designed to work — not whether it can.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Recovery Summary is the **business structure layer**.

Position in system:

Cost Summary
↓
Business Model
↓
**Recovery Summary**
↓
Rate Models
↓
Production Capacity

---

## INPUTS (LOCKED)

### 1. Cost Structure

From:

* Cost Modules
* Cost Summary

Includes:

* labour costs
* material costs
* overheads
* asset costs

---

### 2. Business Model Settings

From:

* Business Model

Includes:

* how the business operates
* structure of work
* earning approach

---

## OUTPUTS (LOCKED)

### 1. Revenue Structure

* how revenue is generated across the business
* distribution between:

  * labour
  * materials
  * other components

---

### 2. Cost Recovery Structure

* how each cost category is recovered
* how the business covers:

  * fixed costs
  * variable costs

---

### 3. Earning Model Clarity

* how the business turns work into revenue
* how different activities contribute to overall performance

---

## KEY FUNCTION

This page answers:

> “How is this business designed to recover its costs through the work it performs?”

---

## CRITICAL DISTINCTION

### Recovery Summary vs Rate Models

* Recovery Summary → defines **earning structure**
* Rate Models → defines **required rates**

---

### Recovery Summary vs Production Capacity

* Recovery Summary → defines **how the business is designed to earn**
* Production Capacity → defines **whether it can realistically deliver that outcome**

---

## WHAT THIS PAGE DOES NOT DO

* does not calculate pricing
* does not define charge rates
* does not assess capacity or time
* does not determine whether the business works

---

## RULES

### 1. No Capacity Logic

This page must NOT include:

* time calculations
* utilisation
* delivery constraints

---

### 2. No Pricing Outputs

This page must NOT:

* produce final charge rates
* define pricing decisions

---

### 3. Structural Clarity Only

Outputs must focus on:

* structure
* relationships
* flow of revenue and cost

---

## INTERPRETATION

The user should be able to see:

* where their revenue is coming from
* how their costs are being recovered
* how their business is structured to operate

---

## DEFINITION

The Recovery Summary page defines the earning structure of the business by showing how costs are recovered through work performed, forming the foundation for pricing and operational analysis.

---

## FINAL LINE

> This is how your business is structured to earn.
