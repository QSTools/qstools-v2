# QS Tools — Quote Benchmark Page (v3.0 — LOCKED)

STATUS: REAL-WORLD VALIDATION LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

To compare real quotes against the business model.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

> The user enters the quote — the system translates it

---

## USER INPUTS

### Quote Basis (selector)

* per m²
* per hour
* total job

---

### Required Inputs

#### Per m²

* total m²
* total quote

---

#### Per hour

* total hours
* total quote

---

#### Total job

* total quote
* optional:

  * m²
  * hours
  * duration

---

## SYSTEM CALCULATIONS

Automatically derive:

* quoted $/m² (if possible)
* quoted $/hour (if possible)

---

Compare against:

* required rate from Rate Models

---

## OUTPUTS

* quoted rate
* required rate
* difference ($ and %)
* benchmark status

---

## STATUS TYPES

* ✔ Above baseline
* ⚠ Close to baseline
* ❌ Below baseline

---

## RULES

* no mental maths required
* no manual conversion required
* user enters quote in their own format

---

## PURPOSE CLARITY

This page does NOT:

* generate the quote
* replace quoting process

---

It DOES:

> show what the quote means to the business

---

## DEFINITION

Quote Benchmark v3.0 converts real-world quotes into comparable rates and benchmarks them against the business model without requiring the user to perform any calculations.
