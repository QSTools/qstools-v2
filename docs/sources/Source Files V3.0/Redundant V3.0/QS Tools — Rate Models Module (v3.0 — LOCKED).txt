# QS Tools — Rate Models Module (v3.0 — LOCKED)

STATUS: EXECUTION LAYER (PACKAGE BUILDER + RATE ENGINE)

---

## PURPOSE

The Rate Models Module converts:

* cost
* recovery
* output

into a usable **sell rate**

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

> You price what you deliver, not what you use

---

## STRUCTURE

Rate Models is built around:

> **Delivery Packages**

---

## DELIVERY PACKAGE

A package represents:

> what it takes to deliver your product

---

### Example — Pump Business

* pump
* ute
* operator
  → output = hours

---

### Example — Foundations Business

* labour (blended)
* asset cost (spread)
* hired equipment (averaged)
  → output = m²

---

## INPUTS

### 1. Delivery Cost (time-based)

* labour_cost_per_period
* asset_cost_per_period
* hired_equipment_cost_per_period

---

### 2. Recovery Requirement

* recovery_required_per_period

---

### 3. Output (MANDATORY)

* output_per_period
  (hours / m² / jobs)

---

## CALCULATIONS

total_required_per_period =
delivery_cost + recovery_required

---

sell_rate =
total_required_per_period ÷ output

---

## OUTPUTS

* sell_rate ($/unit)
* total_required_per_period
* output
* recovery contribution per unit

---

## HIRED EQUIPMENT (LOCKED)

Hired equipment must:

* be entered here
* be treated as delivery cost
* be converted to hourly or unit cost

---

## RULES

* no cost creation
* no strategy logic
* no optimisation
* output must be user-defined

---

## DEFINITION

Rate Models v3.0 is the core revenue engine of QS Tools. It converts delivery cost, recovery requirements, and output into a required sell rate using a package-based model.
