# QS Tools — Active System Brief (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: MASTER SYSTEM CONTROL — FULLY ALIGNED BASELINE

---

## PURPOSE

This brief defines the active QS Tools system after the Phase 1 simplification cleanup.

It exists to:

- keep all future builds aligned
- prevent source drift
- remove deprecated Employee / Staff Overheads module logic
- define clear ownership of cost, recovery, pricing, capacity, and validation layers

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

QS Tools mirrors a business, defines what it must recover, and validates whether real-world work supports that reality.

## PHASE 1 SIMPLIFICATION RULE

Only assign costs where they are stable.

Shared, pooled, unclear, or staff-related overheads must not be distributed during setup.

They belong in General Overheads.

---

## REMOVED MODULE

Employee Overheads / Staff Overheads module is removed completely.

Do not build, reference, wire, or reconcile:

- Employee Overheads module
- employee_overheads_total
- total_employee_overheads_annual
- employee_overheads_benchmark_total
- employee_overheads_ready
- staff overhead per staff logic
- employee overhead joins by staff_id

---

## CORE MODULE ROLES

### P&L

Role:
Input + classification + benchmark layer.

Owns:

- revenue capture
- COGS classification
- business cost classification
- benchmark totals for reconciliation

Does not:

- calculate module totals
- feed raw values directly into Cost Summary
- own downstream cost calculations

---

### Labour

Role:
People cost engine.

Owns:

- staff identity
- wage cost
- entitlements
- employer contributions
- productive hours
- labour-only commercial outputs

Labour does NOT include:

- staff overheads
- admin overheads
- PPE
- tools
- phones
- vehicles
- business overhead loading
- full recovery rate

---

### Assets

Role:
Owned asset cost engine.

Owns:

- asset identity
- finance cost
- direct per-asset running cost
- total asset cost

Assets does NOT include:

- shared fleet fuel
- pooled maintenance
- staff assignment
- cost distribution
- pricing
- recovery

---

### General Overheads

Role:
Business overhead pool.

Owns:

- fixed business overheads
- admin staff cost
- staff overheads
- office/admin costs
- phones
- PPE
- insurance
- facilities
- shared vehicle/fleet costs
- costs that cannot be cleanly assigned during setup

Rule:

If a cost cannot be cleanly assigned during setup, it belongs in General Overheads.

---

### Cost Summary

Role:
Pure cost aggregation layer.

Owns:

- People Cost total
- Business Cost total
- Total Cost Burden
- Required Revenue
- Required Recovery Rate

Cost Summary does NOT:

- rebuild upstream module maths
- consume raw P&L lines
- consume benchmark values in calculations
- consume Employee Overheads
- distribute costs
- define recovery strategy
- define pricing

---

### Recovery Summary

Role:
Business structure / recovery strategy layer.

Owns:

- how cost recovery is structured
- labour/material/asset/overhead recovery logic
- recovery distribution outputs

Does not:

- rebuild upstream costs
- define final charge rates
- assess production capacity

---

### Rate Models

Role:
Pricing layer.

Owns:

- required earning rates
- practical rate formats
- pricing model translation

Does not:

- assess delivery capacity
- validate quotes
- redefine cost burden

---

### Production Capacity

Role:
Operational reality layer.

Owns:

- required time
- available time
- utilisation
- intensity / pressure

Does not:

- price work
- model individual jobs
- create cost totals

---

### Recovery Outcome

Role:
Final business reality layer.

Owns:

- financial reality summary
- pricing reality summary
- production reality summary
- overall business status

Does not:

- prescribe decisions
- calculate new costs
- change upstream logic

---

### Quote Benchmark

Role:
Real-world validation layer.

Owns:

- quote comparison
- quote position
- pricing impact
- capacity impact
- business impact

Does not:

- create quotes
- decide whether to accept work
- replace user judgement

---

## COST STRUCTURE

### People Cost

People Cost now includes Labour only:

- gross wages
- entitlements
- employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- employer contribution total

No staff overheads are included in People Cost.

---

### Business Cost

Business Cost includes:

- Assets
- General Overheads

General Overheads includes staff-related overheads and shared costs.

---

## MODEL READINESS

Model readiness depends on:

- pnl_ready
- labour_ready
- assets_ready
- general_overheads_ready

Employee Overheads readiness is removed.

---

## SYSTEM RULES

- Each cost is entered once
- Each cost is owned by one module
- No module rebuilds another module’s calculations
- No display names are used as keys
- No contract variable names are casually renamed
- No business logic belongs in UI components
- Early modules show stable outputs only
- Full business pressure appears later in Recovery / Rate / Outcome layers

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

QS Tools v3.5 is the fully aligned baseline where Labour owns people employment cost, Assets own asset cost, General Overheads own all shared and business overheads, Cost Summary aggregates stable module totals, and downstream layers define recovery, pricing, capacity, outcome, and quote validation.

---

## FINAL LINE

If it is unstable during setup, do not allocate it — hold it in General Overheads.
