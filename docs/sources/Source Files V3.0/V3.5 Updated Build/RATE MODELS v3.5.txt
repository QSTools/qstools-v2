# QS Tools — Rate Models Page (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: PRICING LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

The Rate Models page converts the business model into real-world earning requirements.

It translates cost and recovery requirements into practical rates.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Rate Models defines how the business must earn — not whether it can deliver it.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Rate Models sits downstream of Recovery Summary and upstream of Production Capacity.

Recovery Summary
↓
Rate Models
↓
Production Capacity

---

## INPUTS

From Cost Summary:

- required_revenue
- total_cost_burden

From Recovery Summary:

- active_recovery_model
- recovery distribution outputs

---

## OUTPUTS

Rate Models may produce:

- required_hourly_rate
- required_m2_rate
- required_unit_rate
- required_crew_week_rate
- revenue_distribution
- recovery_alignment

---

## RULES

Rate Models must NOT:

- calculate capacity
- calculate utilisation
- validate quotes
- prescribe pricing decisions
- rebuild cost totals

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Rate Models v3.5 converts the financial and recovery structure of the business into usable real-world earning rates.
