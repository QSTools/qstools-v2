# QS Tools — Quote Benchmark Page (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: REAL-WORLD VALIDATION LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

The Quote Benchmark page compares a real quote against the business model.

It shows whether quoted work supports or weakens the business.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

The quote is entered in the user’s normal format. The system translates it and shows what it means.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Quote Benchmark is the real-world validation layer.

Recovery Outcome
↓
Quote Benchmark

---

## INPUTS

From Rate Models:

- required rates
- rate structure

From Production Capacity:

- capacity position
- time pressure
- delivery reality

From Recovery Outcome:

- overall business status
- financial / pricing / production reality

From User Quote:

- total job value
- hourly rate
- m2 rate
- unit rate
- relevant production quantity

---

## OUTPUTS

### Quote Position

- Above baseline
- Near baseline
- Below baseline

### Pricing Impact

- meets required rate
- under-recovers
- over-recovers

### Capacity Impact

- manageable
- tight
- unsustainable

### Business Impact

- supports the model
- keeps the business tight
- weakens the model

---

## RULES

Quote Benchmark must NOT:

- create the quote
- decide whether the user takes the job
- prescribe action
- rebuild upstream calculations

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Quote Benchmark v3.5 is the real-world validation layer that shows whether an actual quote supports or weakens the business financially and operationally.
