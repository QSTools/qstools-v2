# QS Tools — Labour Module (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: PEOPLE COST ENGINE

---

## PURPOSE

The Labour Module calculates the cost of employing staff.

It owns:

- staff identity
- wages
- entitlements
- employer contributions
- productive hours
- labour-only charge-out logic

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Labour represents the cost of employing staff only.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Labour is an input module and people cost engine.

It provides stable Labour outputs to Cost Summary.

---

## LABOUR INCLUDES

- gross wages
- paid hours
- non-productive paid hours
- productive hours
- entitlements
- employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- employer contribution total
- labour-only minimum charge-out
- productivity and margin outputs

---

## LABOUR EXCLUDES

Labour must not include:

- staff overheads
- admin staff overheads
- PPE
- phones
- vehicles
- tools
- business overhead loading
- asset costs
- full business recovery rate
- overhead per hour
- true labour cost including overhead

---

## OUTPUT CONTRACT

Per staff:

- staff_id
- staff_name
- staff_role
- labour_class
- paid_hours_per_year
- non_productive_paid_hours
- productive_hours
- gross_wages_annual
- entitlements_annual
- employer_kiwisaver_gross
- esct_amount
- total_employer_contribution_cost
- total_labour_cost_annual
- loaded_labour_cost_rate
- productive_labour_cost_rate
- minimum_charge_out_rate
- actual_margin_percent
- margin_gap
- profit_per_hour
- above_recovery

Aggregate:

- total_gross_wages_annual
- total_entitlements_annual
- total_employer_kiwisaver_annual
- total_esct_annual
- total_employer_contribution_annual
- total_labour_cost_annual
- total_productive_output

---

## RECONCILIATION

Input from P&L:

- labour_benchmark_total

Outputs:

- labour_ready
- labour_variance_amount
- labour_variance_percent
- labour_status

Rules:

- benchmark is reference only
- Labour total remains source of truth
- variance does not flow downstream
- Labour excludes COGS

---

## COST SUMMARY USE

Cost Summary consumes Labour outputs only.

Cost Summary must not rebuild Labour calculations.

---

## UI RULES

Early Labour pages must show:

- stable per-staff cost
- labour-only charge-out
- productivity and margin

Do not show:

- overhead-loaded labour rates
- business recovery rates
- full recovery burden

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

The Labour Module v3.5 is the people cost engine for QS Tools. It calculates the real employment cost and productive labour position while excluding all staff overheads and wider business recovery logic.
