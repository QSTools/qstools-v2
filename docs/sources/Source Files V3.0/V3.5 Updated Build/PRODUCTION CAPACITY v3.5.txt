# QS Tools — Production Capacity Page (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: OPERATIONAL REALITY LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

The Production Capacity page converts required revenue into time and checks whether the business can realistically deliver it.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

Revenue becomes time. Time reveals the truth.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Production Capacity sits downstream of Rate Models and upstream of Recovery Outcome.

Rate Models
↓
Production Capacity
↓
Recovery Outcome

---

## INPUTS

From Cost Summary / Rate Models:

- required_revenue
- production_rate
- available_time

---

## CALCULATIONS

time_required =
required_revenue / production_rate

time_gap =
available_time - time_required

utilisation =
time_required / available_time

---

## OUTPUTS

- time_required
- time_available
- time_gap
- utilisation
- capacity_status
- intensity

---

## CAPACITY STATUS

- capacity_available
- tight
- constrained

---

## RULES

Production Capacity must NOT:

- price work
- validate quotes
- allocate assets
- calculate business cost
- provide solutions

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Production Capacity v3.5 is the operational reality layer that shows whether the business can realistically produce the revenue required by the model.
