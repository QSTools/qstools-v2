# QS Tools — Recovery Outcome Page (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: FINAL BUSINESS REALITY LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

Recovery Outcome brings the system outputs together into one clear view of whether the business model is working, tight, or not working.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

We do not tell the user what to do. We show them what is happening.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

Recovery Outcome sits downstream of:

- Cost Summary
- Recovery Summary
- Rate Models
- Production Capacity

And upstream of:

- Quote Benchmark

---

## INPUTS

### Financial Position

From Cost Summary and Recovery Summary:

- required revenue
- total cost burden
- cost structure
- recovery targets

### Pricing Position

From Rate Models:

- required rates
- earning structure
- rate alignment

### Production Position

From Production Capacity:

- time required
- time available
- utilisation
- intensity
- capacity status

---

## OUTPUT STRUCTURE

### Financial Reality

Shows whether the business generates enough to support itself.

### Pricing Reality

Shows whether the earning structure aligns with required recovery.

### Production Reality

Shows whether the business can realistically deliver the required output.

### Overall Business Status

Allowed statuses:

- Working
- Tight
- Not Working

---

## STATUS LOGIC

Overall status reflects:

- financial viability
- pricing alignment
- production capacity

---

## RULES

Recovery Outcome must NOT:

- prescribe decisions
- recommend specific actions
- calculate new costs
- rebuild upstream outputs
- replace user judgement

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

Recovery Outcome v3.5 is the final business reality layer that reflects financial, pricing, and operational truth before the user applies the model to real-world quotes.
