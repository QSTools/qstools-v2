# QS Tools — P&L Page (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: INPUT + CLASSIFICATION + BENCHMARK LAYER

---

## PURPOSE

The P&L Page captures the real financial position of the business and separates:

- Revenue
- Cost of Goods Sold / delivery costs
- Business costs

It provides benchmark totals for downstream module reconciliation.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

COGS is delivery cost — not business cost.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

P&L is the input classification and benchmark layer.

It does not calculate module totals.

It does not directly drive Cost Summary calculations.

---

## STRUCTURE

### 1. Revenue

- total_revenue

### 2. COGS / Delivery Costs

- materials_cost
- subcontract_cost
- hired_equipment_cost

### 3. Business Costs

Business cost categories:

- labour
- assets
- general_overheads
- unassigned

---

## REMOVED CATEGORY

The following category is removed:

- employee_overheads

Any staff overhead, PPE, phones, admin staff, or shared staff cost must be classified as:

- general_overheads

---

## P&L LINE MODEL

Each P&L line includes:

- pnl_line_id
- line_name
- amount
- section
- category

---

## CATEGORY VALUES

Allowed categories:

- revenue
- cogs_materials
- cogs_subcontract
- cogs_hire
- labour
- assets
- general_overheads
- unassigned

---

## CALCULATIONS

### Total Revenue

total_revenue =
sum(all revenue lines)

### Total COGS

total_cogs =
materials_cost + subcontract_cost + hired_equipment_cost

### Gross Profit

gross_profit =
total_revenue - total_cogs

### Module Benchmark Totals

labour_benchmark_total =
sum(lines where category = labour)

assets_benchmark_total =
sum(lines where category = assets)

general_overheads_benchmark_total =
sum(lines where category = general_overheads)

### Total Business Costs

total_business_costs =
labour_benchmark_total
+ assets_benchmark_total
+ general_overheads_benchmark_total

### Unassigned Balance

unassigned_balance =
sum(lines where category = unassigned)

---

## OUTPUTS

- total_revenue
- total_cogs
- gross_profit
- labour_benchmark_total
- assets_benchmark_total
- general_overheads_benchmark_total
- total_business_costs
- unassigned_balance
- pnl_ready

---

## READINESS RULE

pnl_ready =
unassigned_balance = 0

---

## DOWNSTREAM USE

P&L benchmark totals flow to module reconciliation only.

P&L does not feed Cost Summary calculations directly.

---

## UI STRUCTURE

1. PnLStatusStrip
2. PnLMainCard
3. PnLHelpPanel

---

## UI RULES

- grouped sections allowed
- collapsible sections allowed
- subtotals visible in collapsed headers
- operating expenses may use the Reclassification UI Pattern
- COGS and revenue must remain clearly separate

---

## CORE RULES

- no duplication of cost
- no Employee Overheads category
- no COGS in business costs
- no raw P&L values used directly in Cost Summary
- modules own their own calculations

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

The P&L Page v3.5 is the financial classification and benchmark layer that separates revenue, delivery costs, and business costs, with all staff overheads and shared costs classified under General Overheads.
