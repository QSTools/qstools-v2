# QS Tools — General Overheads Category Mapping Brief (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: STAFF OVERHEADS CATEGORY CLARITY

---

## PURPOSE

This brief clarifies how General Overheads categories work after removing Employee / Staff Overheads as a standalone module.

It exists to prevent confusion between:

- Staff Overheads as a category label
- Employee Overheads as a removed module

---

## CORE RULE

Staff Overheads is a General Overheads category label only.

It is not:

- a standalone module
- a Cost Summary input
- a Labour output
- a staff_id-linked cost layer
- a downstream contract

---

## GENERAL OVERHEADS ROLE

General Overheads owns all business-level shared and fixed overheads, including costs that cannot be cleanly assigned during setup.

---

## CATEGORY GROUPS

General Overheads may use these UI category groups:

- Office / Admin
- Financial Admin
- Insurance & Compliance
- Vehicles (Running)
- Staff Overheads
- Facilities / Premises
- Sales / Growth
- Other / Unallocated

These are grouping labels only.

They do not create new downstream modules.

---

## STAFF OVERHEADS CATEGORY MAY INCLUDE

Examples:

- PPE
- staff phones
- staff training
- staff welfare
- recruitment
- admin support cost
- small tools that are not tracked as assets
- general staff-related operating costs
- costs that support people but are not wages or employer payroll obligations

---

## STAFF OVERHEADS CATEGORY MUST NOT INCLUDE

- gross wages
- paid entitlements
- employer KiwiSaver
- ESCT
- PAYE
- direct Labour module outputs
- direct asset finance costs
- materials
- subcontractors
- hired equipment / COGS

---

## COST SUMMARY TREATMENT

Cost Summary receives only:

- total_general_overheads

It does not receive:

- staff_overheads_total
- employee_overheads_total
- category-specific staff overhead outputs

Staff Overheads contributes only as part of:

- total_general_overheads

---

## P&L TREATMENT

P&L must not use:

- employee_overheads

Any staff overhead P&L line must be classified as:

- general_overheads

The General Overheads page may then visually group it under:

- Staff Overheads

---

## UI RULE

The UI may show Staff Overheads as a collapsible group with a subtotal.

That subtotal is display/review only unless a later locked brief explicitly creates a new output.

---

## RECLASSIFICATION RULE

User may reclassify lines between General Overheads groups.

This does not change the downstream contract.

The downstream output remains:

- total_general_overheads

---

## NOT ALLOWED

- rebuilding an Employee Overheads module
- creating staff_id-linked overhead records
- sending staff overhead totals separately to Cost Summary
- treating Staff Overheads as Labour
- treating Staff Overheads as a separate readiness module
- using category labels as system keys

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

In v3.5, Staff Overheads is only a General Overheads review category. All staff/shared overhead costs roll into total_general_overheads and flow to Cost Summary as part of Business Cost.
