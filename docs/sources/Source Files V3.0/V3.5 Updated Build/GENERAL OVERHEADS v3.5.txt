# QS Tools — General Overheads Module (v3.5 — LOCKED)

STATUS: FULL BUSINESS OVERHEAD POOL

---

## PURPOSE

The General Overheads Module captures all business-level operating costs that are not Labour wage costs, direct owned asset costs, or COGS.

It converts P&L-fed overhead data into a clean structured overhead model.

---

## CORE PRINCIPLE

If a cost cannot be cleanly assigned during setup, it belongs in General Overheads.

---

## SYSTEM ROLE

General Overheads is the fixed and shared business cost layer.

It owns:

- business overhead classification
- staff-related overheads
- shared operating costs
- total_general_overheads

---

## GENERAL OVERHEADS INCLUDE

- admin staff cost
- office/admin costs
- accounting/bookkeeping
- legal fees
- software subscriptions
- phones
- PPE
- insurance
- compliance
- facilities / premises
- sales / growth
- shared vehicle/fleet costs
- shared fuel
- shared maintenance
- unallocated business overheads

---

## GENERAL OVERHEADS EXCLUDE

- wages
- PAYE / ESCT / KiwiSaver employer contribution outputs owned by Labour
- direct asset finance / ownership costs
- materials
- subcontractors
- hired equipment / delivery cost
- COGS

---

## CATEGORY GROUPS

Locked UI groups:

- Office / Admin
- Financial Admin
- Insurance & Compliance
- Vehicles (Running)
- Staff Overheads
- Facilities / Premises
- Sales / Growth
- Other / Unallocated

These are grouping labels, not separate downstream modules.

---

## INPUT BEHAVIOUR

General Overheads may auto-populate from P&L business cost lines.

The user reviews and reclassifies items.

The user should adjust, not re-enter, where P&L source data exists.

---

## RECLASSIFICATION UI

Desktop / tablet:

- drag and drop where supported

Mobile:

- dropdown selector

Each category must:

- be collapsible
- show subtotal in header
- expand to show items

---

## OUTPUT CONTRACT

- total_general_overheads
- category_totals
- general_overheads_benchmark_total
- general_overheads_variance_amount
- general_overheads_variance_percent
- general_overheads_ready

---

## RECONCILIATION

Input from P&L:

- general_overheads_benchmark_total

Rules:

- benchmark is reference only
- module total remains source of truth
- variance does not flow downstream
- excludes COGS

---

## COST SUMMARY USE

Cost Summary consumes:

- total_general_overheads

Cost Summary maps this to:

- total_business_overheads

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

The General Overheads Module v3.5 is the full business overhead pool for QS Tools. It includes staff overheads and shared costs, excludes Labour wage costs and direct Assets, and provides total_general_overheads to Cost Summary.
