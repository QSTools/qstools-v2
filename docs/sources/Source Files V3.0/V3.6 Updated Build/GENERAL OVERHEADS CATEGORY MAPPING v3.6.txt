# QS Tools — General Overheads Category Mapping Brief (v3.6 — LOCKED)

STATUS: STAFF OVERHEADS + VEHICLE RUNNING COST CLARITY

---

## v3.6 ADDITION

General Overheads also owns vehicle and fleet running costs.

These include:

- fuel
- servicing
- repairs
- maintenance
- registration
- licensing
- tyres
- consumables
- shared vehicle costs
- shared fleet costs

These are not Assets module costs.

Assets owns ownership cost only.

---

## ACC CLARITY

General Overheads must not include employer ACC levy.

Employer ACC levy belongs in Labour as an employer payroll obligation.

Insurance and compliance costs may remain in General Overheads, but employer ACC levy must not be grouped into Insurance & Compliance.

---

## FINAL DEFINITION

In v3.6, Staff Overheads remains only a General Overheads review category, and vehicle running costs are explicitly owned by General Overheads. All shared/staff/vehicle overhead categories roll into total_general_overheads and flow to Cost Summary as part of Business Cost.
