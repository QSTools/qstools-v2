## SYSTEM ROLE CLARITY (CRITICAL)

### Cost Summary
- Aggregates all business costs
- Produces total_cost_burden
- Produces required_recovery_rate
- Defines WHAT the business costs

---

### Recovery Summary (NEW LAYER)
- Consumes Cost Summary outputs only
- Defines recovery strategy
- Distributes cost across:
  - labour
  - assets
  - overhead absorption
- Produces recovery targets
- Defines HOW the business recovers cost

---

### Cost Allocation (UPDATED ROLE)
- Consumes Recovery Summary outputs
- Consumes Labour + Asset identities
- Defines:
  - asset ↔ labour capability links
  - operational groups (delivery units)
- Validates:
  - structural readiness
  - operational completeness
- Does NOT define recovery strategy
- Does NOT calculate cost or recovery

---

## CORE SYSTEM PRINCIPLE (UPDATED)

Cost Summary = WHAT it costs  
Recovery Summary = HOW it is recovered  
Cost Allocation = CAN it be delivered
