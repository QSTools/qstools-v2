QS Tools — Demo Build & Deployment Brief (v1.0 — LOCKED)
🎯 Purpose

The QS Tools Demo is designed to:

create a realisation moment
expose the gap between perceived rate vs actual working rate
drive users to ask:

“What should I actually be charging?”

The demo is:

NOT the full system
NOT a precision calculator
a problem awareness engine

Each calculator must be on its own page for:
👉 usage tracking
👉 flow measurement

🔁 Core User Flow (LOCKED)

Entry points:

Square Metre Rate
Materials + Labour
Labour Reality

Primary flow:

Entry calculators → Labour Reality

🧮 Calculator Roles
Square Metre Rate
Shows: gross hourly outcome
Based on: production assumptions

Message:

“This is what your job is producing”

👉 CTA → Labour Reality

Materials + Labour
Shows: gross job-based hourly outcome
Based on: job pricing structure

Message:

“This is your gross working outcome”

👉 CTA → Labour Reality

Labour Reality (CORE PAGE)
Shows: adjusted working rate
Includes:
non-productive time
entitlements

👉 Delivers:
“oh shit” moment

⚠️ Critical Rule

Labour Reality MUST state:

This is NOT take-home pay
Tax, ACC, ESCT are NOT included

👉 These are referenced but NOT calculated

💬 Core Messaging
Entry Calculators

“This is your gross hourly outcome.
Check what this actually means in Labour Reality.”

Labour Reality

“This rate already allows for non-productive time.
It still does not represent take-home pay — tax and business costs still come out of this.”

🧠 Positioning
Entry calculators = what the job produces
Labour Reality = what you actually earn
