# QS Tools — Business Performance Page (v1.0 — LOCKED)

STATUS: REALITY VS TRUSTED MODEL PAGE

---

## PURPOSE

The Business Performance Page compares actual business performance against the trusted cost model.

It exists to answer:

- are you recovering your business cost?
- how does actual revenue compare to required revenue?
- how much real-world gap exists between the business and the trusted model?

This page does NOT:

- assign P&L line items
- reconcile modules
- build cost structure
- perform pricing logic

---

## SYSTEM ROLE (CRITICAL)

The Business Performance Page is a:

summary comparison layer

Position:

P&L MAPPING PAGE
↓
MODULE RECONCILIATION
↓
COST SUMMARY
↓
BUSINESS PERFORMANCE PAGE

The page must:

- consume actual revenue-side inputs
- consume trusted Cost Summary outputs
- compare real-world performance to required business baseline

## INPUTS

### From P&L

- total_revenue
- materials_cost
- subcontract_cost

### From Cost Summary

- required_revenue
- total_cost_burden
- required_recovery_rate
- total_productive_output

---

## CALCULATIONS

gross_profit =
total_revenue - materials_cost - subcontract_cost

recovery_gap =
total_revenue - required_revenue

recovery_ratio =
total_revenue / required_revenue

actual_recovery_rate =
total_revenue / total_productive_output

---

## OUTPUTS

- gross_profit
- recovery_gap
- recovery_ratio
- actual_recovery_rate

---

## DEFINITION

The Business Performance Page is the reality-versus-model page of QS Tools. It compares actual revenue-side performance against the trusted cost model so the user can see whether the business is truly recovering its required cost base.
