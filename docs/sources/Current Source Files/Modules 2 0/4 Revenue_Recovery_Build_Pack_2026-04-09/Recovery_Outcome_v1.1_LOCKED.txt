QS Tools — Recovery Outcome (v1.1 — LOCKED)
PURPOSE

Recovery Outcome is the macro business reconciliation page.

It combines:

cost baseline
recovery strategy
materials contribution
rate-model contribution
structural readiness

It answers:

are we actually recovering enough
what streams are carrying the business
what stream is missing
is the business model working overall
SYSTEM ROLE (CRITICAL)

Recovery Outcome is a:

macro recovery reconciliation + decision layer

It must:

consume Recovery Summary outputs
consume Cost Allocation outputs
consume Materials outputs
consume Rate Model outputs
produce the overall business verdict

It must NOT:

rebuild Cost Summary maths
rebuild Recovery Summary maths
rebuild Cost Allocation logic
become a detailed quoting page
CORE PRINCIPLE

Cost Summary = what it costs
Recovery Summary = how recovery is intended
Materials = material margin contribution
Rate Models = production-based revenue contribution
Cost Allocation = structural delivery readiness
Recovery Outcome = does the model actually work
