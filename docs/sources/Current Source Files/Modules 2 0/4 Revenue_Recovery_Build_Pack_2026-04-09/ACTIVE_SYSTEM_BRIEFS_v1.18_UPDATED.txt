SYSTEM ROLE CLARITY (UPDATED)
Cost Summary
aggregates all business costs
produces total_cost_burden
produces required_revenue
produces required_recovery_rate baseline
defines WHAT the business costs
Recovery Summary
consumes Cost Summary outputs only
defines recovery strategy
distributes intended recovery across labour, assets, and overhead absorption
defines HOW cost is intended to be recovered
Materials
captures annual material cost
captures annual material revenue
calculates annual material margin
defines material margin contribution to business recovery
Rate Models
define what the business physically sells
define sellable units such as:
square metres
tonnes
cubic metres
loads
define minimum-charge and volume-based rate logic
define expected production-based recovery contribution
Cost Allocation
consumes Recovery Summary outputs
consumes Labour + Asset identities and outputs
defines capability links and operational groups
shows micro operating-unit health
defines CAN it be delivered as configured
Recovery Outcome
consumes Recovery Summary outputs
consumes Materials outputs
consumes Rate Model outputs
consumes Cost Allocation outputs
produces the macro business verdict
defines DOES the whole business model actually work
CORE SYSTEM PRINCIPLE (UPDATED)

Cost Summary = WHAT it costs
Recovery Summary = HOW it is intended to be recovered
Materials = WHAT material margin contributes
Rate Models = WHAT is physically sold / produced
Cost Allocation = CAN it be delivered
Recovery Outcome = DOES it actually work

IMPORTANT SYSTEM CHANGE

QS Tools is no longer only a cost-and-recovery tool.

It is now a production-based recovery system.

It must answer:

what the business costs
how recovery is intended
what actually generates revenue
whether the operating structure can deliver that model
whether the full business model is commercially viable
