P&L Page — UI Layout Map v1.0

Page purpose

Annual business truth entry page.

This is the user’s real-world starting point.

It captures the high-level business picture from the last financial year before the user moves into deeper cost, recovery, revenue-stream, and decision pages.

File structure

components/p-and-l/

ProfitAndLossStatusStrip.jsx
ProfitAndLossMainCard.jsx
ProfitAndLossSummaryCard.jsx
ProfitAndLossHelpPanel.jsx

Optional later:
ProfitAndLossCustomLinesCard.jsx

Page order

ProfitAndLossStatusStrip
ProfitAndLossMainCard
ProfitAndLossSummaryCard
ProfitAndLossHelpPanel

1. ProfitAndLossStatusStrip

Show:

profile active state
annual_total_revenue
annual_total_cost
annual_operating_result
warning count

Status pills

Revenue entered / missing
Cost entered / missing
Operating result positive / negative / zero

Status intent

Quick top-line answer:

“Has the annual business picture been entered, and does it broadly make sense?”

2. ProfitAndLossMainCard

Section A — Page intro

Title:
P&L Business Picture

Short explanation:
“Enter your annual business totals from the last financial year to build the starting picture of how your business actually performed.”

Optional support text:
“This page is the simple starting point of QS Tools. It shows what happened in your business before deeper modelling begins.”

---

Section B — Annual revenue inputs

Fields:

annual_labour_revenue
annual_material_revenue
annual_subcontract_revenue
annual_other_revenue

Layout:

stacked on mobile
two-column split on larger screens only if clean and readable

Intent:

Show the main revenue streams the business actually relied on.

---

Section C — Annual cost inputs

Fields:

annual_labour_cost
annual_material_cost
annual_subcontract_cost
annual_overhead_cost
annual_other_cost

Layout:

stacked on mobile
two-column split on larger screens only if clean and readable

Intent:

Keep this simple and annual only.
Do not overload the page with detailed cost breakdown logic.

---

Section D — Optional custom lines

Collapsed by default.

Uses:
ProfitAndLossCustomLinesCard later if needed

Allow:

add custom annual revenue line
add custom annual cost line
assign category
activate / deactivate line

Purpose:

Capture business-specific P&L items without breaking the simple core structure.

Important note:

This section must feel optional, not required.

---

Section E — Live annual picture

Read-only display:

annual_total_revenue
annual_total_cost
annual_operating_result

Intent:

Give the user an immediate business truth summary.

This should feel simple, clear, and calm.

---

Section F — Revenue mix preview

Read-only:

labour_revenue_percent
material_revenue_percent
subcontract_revenue_percent

Optional note examples:

“Material revenue is the dominant stream.”
“Labour revenue is carrying most of the business.”
“This business appears heavily dependent on subcontract revenue.”

Intent:

Help the user see what the business is actually relying on.

This is one of the page’s most important insights.

---

Section G — Simple warning block

Examples:

No revenue entered
No cost entered
Operating result is negative
Material revenue entered but no material cost entered
Labour revenue entered but no labour cost entered
Revenue mix appears highly concentrated in one stream

Intent:

Keep warnings helpful, not noisy.

3. ProfitAndLossSummaryCard

Headline values:

Annual Total Revenue
Annual Total Cost
Operating Result

Secondary values:

Labour Revenue %
Material Revenue %
Subcontract Revenue %

Visual emphasis

Use the same visual language as other QS Tools summary cards:

bad if annual operating result is negative
ok if result is very thin
good if result is healthy

Optional insight block

Examples:

“This page shows what actually happened in the business.”
“Your business appears material-led.”
“Your business appears labour-led.”
“This annual picture is the starting point only — deeper modules refine the model.”

Intent

This card should feel like a simple annual business snapshot.

Not a technical analysis engine.

4. ProfitAndLossHelpPanel

Collapsed by default.

Must explain:

this page is annual only
this page is based on the last financial year
this is the starting point of QS Tools
this page does not replace Labour, Materials, Cost Summary, or Rate Models
this page is not a quote builder
if you do not have a full P&L yet, you can start with Labour and build forward from there

Recommended tone

Simple
plain English
welcoming
non-technical

Recommended page feel

Calm
clear
accounting-like
low-pressure
“start here” feel

This page should not feel like:

a complex model
a quote builder
a technical finance dashboard

It should feel like:

the easiest way into the system

UI intent summary

P&L Page should feel like:

Step 1 — understand what happened

It is the entry page that helps the user move from:

“I know my business is busy”
to
“I can now see what my business actually did over the year”

Strong layout rule

Macro first
detail second
optional complexity collapsed

The user must be able to understand the page without opening any optional sections.
