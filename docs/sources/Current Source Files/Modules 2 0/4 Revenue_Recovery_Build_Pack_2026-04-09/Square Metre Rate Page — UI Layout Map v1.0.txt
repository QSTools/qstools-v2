1. SquareMetreRateStatusStrip

Show:

base rate
minimum charge
expected annual m²
expected annual revenue
rate health / warning count
Status intent

Immediate check that the rate model is populated and believable.

2. SquareMetreRateMainCard
Section A — Page intro

Short explanation:
“This page models revenue generated from m²-based pricing.”

Section B — Core rate inputs

Fields:

base_square_metre_rate
minimum_charge
expected_annual_square_metres
Section C — Small-job / large-job behaviour

Collapsed by default.

Fields:

small_job_threshold_m2
small_job_uplift_percent
large_job_discount_percent
average_job_size_m2
Section D — Optional basis labels

Display-only or light-input depending on your final choice:

included_labour_basis_label
included_material_basis_label

This is for clarity only, not cost ownership.

Section E — Revenue preview

Read-only:

rate_model_type
unit_label
expected_annual_volume
expected_revenue_annual
expected_recovery_contribution
Section F — warnings

Examples:

annual volume missing
base rate missing
minimum charge not entered
very low annual revenue relative to expected business scale
3. SquareMetreRateSummaryCard

Headline values:

Rate per m²

Expected Annual Revenue
Optional insight line

Examples:

“Small jobs likely depend heavily on minimum charge.”

4. SquareMetreRateHelpPanel

Collapsed by default.

Explain:

what this page is for
why small jobs need higher effective rates
why minimum charge matters
this is not a detailed quote builder
this is annual production/revenue modelling only
