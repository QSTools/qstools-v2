QS Tools — Materials Module (v1.0 — LOCKED)

STATUS: NEW RECOVERY STREAM MODULE

PURPOSE

The Materials Module is the material margin layer of QS Tools.

It is responsible for:

capturing annual material cost
capturing annual material revenue
calculating annual material margin
exposing material recovery contribution downstream

This module exists because many businesses recover a significant portion of their total cost through material margin.

CALCULATIONS (LOCKED)

material_margin_annual =
annual_material_revenue - annual_material_cost

material_margin_percent =
material_margin_annual / annual_material_revenue

If annual_material_revenue = 0:

material_margin_percent = 0

DEFINITION

The Materials Module is the annual material margin layer of QS Tools. It defines how much value is bought, how much is sold, and how much margin contributes to recovering total business cost.
