IMPORTANT P&L RULE

P&L Page is the user entry layer.

It:

captures annual business performance  
captures revenue structure  
captures high-level cost structure  

It does NOT:

replace Cost Summary  
replace Materials  
replace Rate Models  
replace Recovery Outcome  

It must remain:

simple  
annual  
non-technical  

---

IMPORTANT COST SUMMARY RULE

Cost Summary remains a pure cost layer.

It:

consumes upstream cost outputs  
produces total_cost_burden  
produces required_revenue  
produces required_recovery_rate baseline  

It does NOT:

define recovery strategy  
define materials margin  
define rate model logic  
consume Cost Allocation outputs  
consume Recovery Outcome outputs  

---

IMPORTANT RECOVERY RULE

Recovery Summary remains the owner of:

recovery model

required recovery outputs  

It defines recovery intent only.

It does not define:

materials margin  
rate model production  
final business verdict

NEW — RECOVERY PERFORMANCE LAYER

Recovery Outcome now includes:

recovered_hours  
actual_productivity_percent  
productivity_gap_hours  

Purpose:

Expose:

under-recovery of labour capacity  
hidden inefficiencies  
dependency on specific revenue streams  

---

RECOVERY PERFORMANCE DEFINITION

Recovered Hours:

labour_revenue ÷ average_charge_out_rate  

Actual Productivity:

recovered_hours ÷ total_productive_output  

Productivity Gap:

total_productive_output − recovered_hours
