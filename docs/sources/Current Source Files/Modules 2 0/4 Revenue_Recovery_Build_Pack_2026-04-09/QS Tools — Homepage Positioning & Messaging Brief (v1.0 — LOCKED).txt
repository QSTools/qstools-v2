Most businesses:

think they know their rate

💬 Messaging Structure
1. Headline (must hit immediately)

Examples:

Do you actually know what your work earns you?

Most people charging by the hour or quoting jobs are earning less than they think.

Whether you charge hourly or quote jobs, your labour still needs to be recovered properly. Most small operators don’t realise how tight their real rate actually is.

3. Problem Section

Explain simply:

You charge $X
You think you earn $Y
But once you allow for:
downtime

inefficiency

4. “Who This Is For” Section

This tool is for:

tradies
contractors
service businesses
anyone quoting jobs or charging for work

Simple 3-step:

Choose how you price your work

6. Calculator Entry Section (CRITICAL)

Three options:

Square Metre Rate

Labour Reality

Each with:

short description
CTA button
7. Key Insight Section

Most businesses don’t lose money on jobs —
they lose it slowly by under-recovering labour.

8. Reality Statement (very important)

This demo doesn’t calculate everything — it shows you the gap.
