QS Tools — Square Metre Rate Page (v1.0 — LOCKED)
PURPOSE

The Square Metre Rate Page is for labour-led or supply-and-install businesses that recover revenue through m²-based pricing.

Examples:

concrete placing
plasterboard fixing
stopping
slab work
surface preparation trades
SYSTEM ROLE (CRITICAL)

This page is a:

production rate model page

It must:

model m²-based revenue
support minimum charge logic
support small-job uplift logic
support expected annual volume
calculate expected annual recovery contribution

It must NOT:

rebuild Labour cost logic
rebuild Materials cost logic
rebuild Cost Summary totals

CORE RULES
must support minimum charge visibility
must explain that small jobs often require higher effective rates
must remain production/revenue focused
must not become a detailed quote builder in v1.0
DEFINITION

The Square Metre Rate Page models trades that recover business cost through m²-based pricing and helps test whether expected production volume and rate structure are sufficient to support the business.
