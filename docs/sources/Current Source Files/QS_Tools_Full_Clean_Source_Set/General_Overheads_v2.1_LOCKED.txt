# General Overheads — Source of Truth (v2.1 — LOCKED)

---

## Purpose

The General Overheads Module captures all annual business-level operating costs that are not directly tied to:

* staff (Labour)
* staff overheads (Employee Overheads)
* assets (Assets Module)
* jobs or revenue generation

It defines the total fixed business overhead burden.

---

## 🔒 SYSTEM ROLE (CRITICAL)

The General Overheads Module is a:

fixed annual input layer

It must:

* capture true annual business costs
* remain simple and input-driven
* provide a single total overhead value

It must NOT:

* perform allocation
* perform recovery calculations
* link to staff or assets
* simulate scenarios
