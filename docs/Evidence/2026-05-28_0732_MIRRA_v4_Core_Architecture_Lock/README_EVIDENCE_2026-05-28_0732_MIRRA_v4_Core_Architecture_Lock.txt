Purpose:
This evidence pack records the retrospective architecture lock for MIRRA v4.0.

This milestone captures the shift from a page/module build into a closed-loop business recovery system.

Core product principle:
Mirra is a closed-loop business recovery model.

The core loop is:

Monthly P&L
↓
Business recovery model
↓
Required labour / asset / overhead / COG recovery
↓
Rate targets
↓
Quote checker
↓
Work orders / purchase orders / time / invoicing
↓
Job performance
↓
Monthly P&L comparison
↓
Model feedback
↓
Approved model improvement
↓
Future quote targets
